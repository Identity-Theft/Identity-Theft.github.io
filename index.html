Minecraft Mods

No XP Anvils

Removes the XP cost for repairing, renaming and applying enchantments to tools and gear in an anvil.

Keep XP on Death

Keep your experience points on death. Port of mastrocaleb's Keep XP on Death to modern versions of Minecraft.

Infinity Rework

Reworks and balances the Infinity enchantment by splitting it into three tiers with each having a higher chance of saving an arrow and allowing bows to be enchanted with both Infinity and Mending. Every feature is fully configurable.

Rail Destinations

Fabric port of the RailSwitch plugin used on the CivMC server that allows rails to automatically switch directions based on a specified destination.

Armour Sound Tweak Updated

A small client-side mod for Fabric that plays the respective equip sound when unequipping armour in a menu. Updated from the mod by HeckinChloe.

Bleached

Adds bleach which can be used to return wool, carpet, beds, banners and leather armour to their original colours.

Sculk Items

Sculk Items was planned to add more to the Ancient Cities but is no longer in development.

Minecraft Modpacks

Pokécraft: Adventures

Experience the world of Pokémon inside minecraft with the PokéCraft: Adventures modpack. Filled with vanilla+ mods that give you a vanilla like experience with a focus on exploration and journey, experience the living world of Cobblemon in this carefully curated modpack.

Automation and Pollution

Automation and Pollution is a lightweight modpack focused on building machinery while also watching out for your carbon and sulfur emissions. This modpack contains new content such as custom generation, structures, more crops and food and heaps of new decorations.

Pizza-Auto

Bean Bot

General purpose Discord bot written in TypeScript using discord.js. Currently, has several music and info commands.
